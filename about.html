About Us

Welcome to Sports For Revival Of the orphanage and the Under Previleged Hope (SROUH)

Sports for Revival of the Orphanage and the Underprivileged Hope (SROUH) is a charitable foundation dedicated to transforming the lives of orphaned and underprivileged children through the power of sports. We believe that sports are more than just games—they are a tool for empowerment, discipline, teamwork, and personal growth.

At SROUH, we provide children with access to structured sports programs, mentorship, education, and life skills that help them build confidence, resilience, and a sense of belonging.
